## The Problem

You have a city center waterfront site. Surrounded by offices, residential towers, and small retail. The brief says "make it better for the community."

But what does "better" actually mean? 15 architects will give you 15 different answers.

Here are 15 programmatic directions — each distilled into a Studio Nomad-ready prompt. No guesswork. Just copy the prompt, adjust the site boundary, and generate.

---

## 15 Schemes

### 01. Urban Memory Corridor + Waterfront Civic Celebration Plaza

A composite cultural public space with memory wall, sculpture clusters, light installations, and a central celebration plaza. Waterfront features a floating theater and open-air performance center.

**Prompt injection:**
```
Urban Memory Corridor + Waterfront Civic Celebration Plaza
Key elements: cultural memory wall, themed landscape, sculpture clusters, 
light art installations, celebration plaza, sunken activity space, 
floating riverside theater, waterside viewing platforms
```

### 02. Riverside Wedding Art Center

Elegant ceremony spaces with riverside terraces, floral landscape, and romantic evening lighting. Designed for after-work events.

**Prompt injection:**
```
Riverside Wedding Art Center
Key elements: ceremony hall, celebration terraces, floral landscape, 
romantic lighting, waterfront backdrop
```

### 03. Waterfront Water Sports Club

Paddleboarding and kayaking facilities with boat docks, equipment storage, and training areas. Active recreation on the water.

**Prompt injection:**
```
Waterfront Water Sports Club
Key elements: paddleboard docks, kayak launch, equipment storage, 
training areas, waterfront recreation deck
```

### 04. Industrial Heritage Revitalization

Adaptive reuse of old factory structures. Exhibition spaces, public art interventions, and industrial memory preservation.

**Prompt injection:**
```
Industrial Heritage Revitalization Zone
Key elements: factory building reuse, exhibition halls, public art, 
industrial memory preservation, creative transformation
```

### 05. Elevated High Line Park + Underground Parking

Multi-level vertical design: green elevated walkway above, parking beneath. Seamless urban connection without surface cars.

**Prompt injection:**
```
Elevated Urban High Line Park + Underground Parking
Key elements: elevated green walkway, underground parking, 
vertical landscape, urban connector
```

## Base Prompt Template

Use this template with any scheme above. Replace the bracketed sections:

```
Transform a city center waterfront site surrounded by office buildings 
and residential areas with small commercial venues. Site is adjacent 
to river with CBD urban architecture in background. Create a 
[SCHEME_NAME] with [KEY_ELEMENTS].

Modern and grand atmosphere, strong cultural ambiance, clean minimalist 
architectural landscape, rich night lighting layers, natural light 
penetration, ecological greening accents. Cinematic quality, HDR high 
definition, ultra-detailed, professional municipal landscape rendering. 
Urban waterfront cultural landmark.
```
